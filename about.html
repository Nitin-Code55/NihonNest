Our Story

From an SSW Truck Driver to building a community for Indians in Japan.

Main khud SSW se Japan gaya.

"Hi, I'm the founder of NihonNest. I currently work as a truck driver in Japan on an SSW visa. Jab main India se plan kar raha tha, I realized how confusing and scary the process is. Agencies asking for lakhs of rupees, fake job promises, and no one to help with accommodation once you land."

"I went through it all. I studied JLPT N4, applied for jobs, figured out the paperwork, and finally landed here. Par yahan aake bhi struggle tha—apartment lene ke liye guarantor chahiye, aur duty ke baad khana banana mushkil hota tha."

"NihonNest isliye banaya kyunki main chahta hoon ki jo struggle maine dekha, wo kisi aur Indian bhai-behen ko na dekhna pade. We provide verified jobs, a ready-to-move room, and Indian food from Day 1."

Our Mission

To make Japan the most accessible, safe, and transparent destination for Indian workers by providing end-to-end guidance, verified employment, and a welcoming community.

The Journey

Started as a Student

Began studying Japanese from scratch in India, aiming for a better future.

Cleared JLPT N4

Passed the JLPT N4 and the SSW skill exams for the transport sector.

Applied for SSW Visa

Navigated the complex visa and paperwork process without an expensive agent.

Landed in Japan

Started working as a Truck Driver. Faced initial struggles with housing and food.

Started NihonNest

Launched this platform to help others settle in Japan smoothly.
